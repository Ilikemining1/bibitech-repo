  • iDroid Project Donations

    Donations to the iDroid Project help us acquire more devices to port OpeniBoot and iDroid. They also help fund our hosting costs, certificates for signing drivers and tools used for porting. If you would like to see development continue and motivate the developers behind the project, please consider donating to one of the developers below.


    So far we have managed to acquire an iPhone 3GS for our developer Ricky to allow him to start the porting process as soon as it arrives.

    iPod Touch 4G - None of the team posess an iPod Touch 4th Generation. As such Bluerise is willing to port OpeniBoot to the device if enough donations are raised. This will also aid iPhone 4 and iPad porting due to the A4 chip inside.
