Frequently Asked Questions
  • I successfully installed iDroid but when I reboot the Apple logo appears and I am returned straight to iOS, where's iDroid/OpeniBoot?

    You probably haven't installed OpeniBoot. Tap on the OpeniBoot tab and install it.


    Does Bootlace support the iPad/iPod Touch 2G/3G/iPhone 3GS/4/my iguana?

    No. Bootlace only supports devices supported by OpeniBoot and by iDroid. As soon as these projects progress to support other devices then I will endeavour to support. To make that clear Bootlace has no special capability to support newer devices and never will.


    I get "Kernelcache appears to be incorrectly patched. Please reinstall Bootlace." but reinstalling doesn't fix it. What do I do?

    Most likely you restored a backup of settings on a fresh restore, upgraded your firmware or re-jailbroke your device. If this is the case, remove the file /var/mobile/Library/Preferences/be.dawson.bootlace.plist then relaunch the app. Do not try this for any other errors. It will NOT help.
